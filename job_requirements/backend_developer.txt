**Required:**

- Server-side language (Node.js/Go/Java/Python)
- Databases (SQL or NoSQL)
- RESTful API design

- Build and maintain server-side services
- Optimize DB queries and design schemas
- Ensure reliability and observability

node, go, express, gin, sql, mongodb, kafka, redis, docker, postgresql, terraform, ci/cd
